Exposure times: what exposure times?

This is an experimental view of the exposure times of the Science observations made by Chandra. That is, how long does Chandra spend observing a target in a single observation, rather than a look at how much time is spent on each target. I may well switch to a histogram view, but the cumulative view means I don't have to think about a sensible binning scheme. I also need to tweak the X axis to display in hours rather than kilo-seconds: 100 kiloseconds is almost 28 hours.

The data used is for all the data shown in the calendar view, and is split by proposal cycle, as well as there being one curve for all the data. I am not going to tell you what the colors mean as I haven't coded that up yet, so don't know myself. I can tell you that the dotted line is for all the data, and that hovering over one line will highlight it.

I think the two curves which are significantly different to the rest are for cycles for which I do not have a lot of data. A legend would help clear this up!
